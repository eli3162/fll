    About us

    We are a First Lego League [FLL] Team from Eastern PA currently participating the FLL Unearthed Season, and is headed to WORLDS in Houston, Texas. This github account is deticated to our team's code, both for Innovation Project and Robot Game.

    FLL

    Innovation Project

    Our innovation solution is Ancient Vision, a state of the art scanner designed to save archeologists lots of time while documenting and storing artifacts, as well as storing them more efficiently using 3d models, as precise as 0.2 mm.
